Privacy Policy for Lotus Forum Thread Exporter

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

Lotus Forum Thread Exporter is a Chrome extension that allows users to export forum threads from lotus-forum.de as PDF documents. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Data Collected from Forum Pages

  • Thread Content: Text content from forum posts, including author names, post dates, and message content
  • Thread Metadata: Thread titles, URLs, post counts, and pagination information
  • Attachments: Information about file attachments in posts (filenames, types, URLs)
  • Quotes: Quoted content from other posts within the thread

Data Stored Locally

  • Export History: Metadata about your previous exports (titles, dates, post counts) stored in Chrome's local storage
  • Settings: Your extension preferences and configuration options
  • PDF Files: Generated PDF files may be temporarily stored in your browser's IndexedDB for download purposes

How We Use Your Information

  • PDF Generation: To create PDF documents from forum thread content
  • Export History: To provide you with a list of your previous exports for re-downloading
  • Extension Functionality: To maintain your preferences and settings

Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third parties. All data processing occurs locally on your device or within the Chrome extension environment. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage APIs
  • PDF files are temporarily stored in your browser's IndexedDB and can be cleared at any time
  • No data is transmitted over the internet except for the initial forum page requests (which are normal web browsing)
  • You can clear all stored data through the extension's settings or Chrome's extension management

Your Rights and Controls

  • Clear Data: You can clear all stored export history and settings through the extension interface
  • Disable Extension: You can disable or remove the extension at any time through Chrome's extension management
  • No Account Required: The extension works without requiring any account or personal information

Permissions Used

This extension uses the following Chrome permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current forum page content for scraping
  • storage: To save your export history and settings locally
  • downloads: To automatically download generated PDF files
  • unlimitedStorage: To store large PDF files and export history

Third-Party Libraries

This extension uses the following third-party libraries:

  • jsPDF: For PDF generation (included locally, no external requests)

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's updated version and posted here. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension's data practices, please contact us through:

  • GitHub Issues: Extension Repository

Compliance

This extension is designed to comply with Chrome Web Store policies and applicable privacy laws. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

This privacy policy is effective as of the date of the extension's publication and applies to all users of the Lotus Forum Thread Exporter Chrome extension.
